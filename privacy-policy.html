Privacy Policy

Welcome to our Privacy Policy. Your privacy is important to us, especially as it relates to the development of software for business needs.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") outlines how we collect, protect, and use the personal information ("Personal Information") you ("User", "you", or "your") provide on our website, which focuses on the development of software for business needs. This Policy does not apply to other practices or websites not related to our business software development services.

We receive and store any information you knowingly provide to us when you fill out online forms on the Website. You can choose not to provide certain information, but doing so may limit your access to specific features related to our software development services.

3. Collection of Non-Personal Information

When you visit our Website, our servers automatically record information your browser sends. This data may include details such as your device's IP address, browser type, operating system, language preferences, or the webpage you were visiting before arriving at our site, as well as information about your interactions with our software development services.

We may use the information we collect from you to improve the user experience, enhance our software development services, and address inquiries. We may use cookies to collect, store, and track information for statistical purposes and to operate our website and services. You have the option to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if preferred.

6. Security of Your Information

We take the security of your information seriously and store it in a secure environment protected from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
